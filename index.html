FAQ

What makes your produce "fresh" and "green"?

We prioritize sourcing our produce directly from local farms and growers whenever possible. This ensures the shortest possible time between harvest and shelf, maximizing freshness and nutritional value. "Green" to us means more than just the color; it represents our commitment to sustainable farming practices, reducing our environmental impact, and offering produce grown with minimal or no pesticides. We clearly label the source of our produce so you know exactly where it comes from.

Do you offer organic options?

Yes, we carry a wide selection of certified organic fruits, vegetables, and other grocery items. We understand the importance of organic produce for many of our customers and strive to provide a diverse range of choices. We also clearly label our organic products for easy identification.

How do you ensure produce quality and safety?

We have strict quality control measures in place. Our team inspects all incoming produce for freshness, ripeness, and any signs of damage. We also work closely with our suppliers to ensure they adhere to safe and sustainable farming practices. We regularly clean and sanitize our produce section and maintain proper storage temperatures to preserve freshness.

Do you offer delivery or curbside pickup?

Yes, we offer both convenient delivery and curbside pickup options for your grocery orders. You can easily place your order online through our website or app, and choose a delivery time that suits you. For curbside pickup, simply select that option at checkout, and we'll bring your order out to your car when you arrive.

What if I'm not happy with the produce I purchased?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, please bring it back with your receipt, and we'll be happy to offer a refund or exchange. We value your feedback and use it to continually improve our products and services.

Do you carry other healthy grocery items besides produce?

Absolutely! In addition to our fresh produce, we offer a wide range of healthy grocery items, including whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats, dairy and non-dairy alternatives, snacks with minimal processed ingredients, and a selection of locally made goods. We aim to be a one-stop shop for all your healthy and wholesome grocery needs.
